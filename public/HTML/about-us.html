About Tifliso Restaurant

Authentic Georgian Cuisine in Budapest

Our Story

The story of Tifliso Restaurant is inspired by Georgia's rich food culture, where meals serve not only to nourish but also to celebrate connection, fellowship, and hospitality. Our goal is to share this tradition with Budapest by offering a dining experience that reflects the true spirit of Georgian cuisine.

From the beginning, Tifliso was created to serve authentic Georgian meals prepared using traditional family recipes, carefully preserved and passed down through generations. each dish telling its own tale about Georgia's rich history, culture, and passion for good food.

Georgian Wine & Traditional Drinks

No Georgian dining experience is complete without wine. Georgia is one of the oldest wine producing regions in the world, and at Tifliso Restaurant offer a carefully selected range of authentic Georgian wines. Each wine is chosen to perfectly complement our dishes and enhance your dining experience.

We also serve traditional Georgian chacha, allowing guests to explore the depth of Georgian beverage culture alongside our cuisine. If you are searching for Georgian wine in Budapest, Tifliso Restaurant should be at the top of your list!
