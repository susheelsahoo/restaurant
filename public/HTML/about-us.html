Authentic Georgian Cuisine

At Tifliso, our mission is to bring authentic Georgian culinary traditions to the heart of Budapest. Our chefs preserve centuries-old recipes while infusing modern culinary artistry.

Each dish reflects our deep respect for Georgian culture, hospitality, and craftsmanship.
