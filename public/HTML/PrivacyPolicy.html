Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us and we are committed to protecting it.

Introduction

At Tifliso Georgian Restaurant, customer satisfaction is very important to us. Our policies are designed to be fair, transparent, and respectful of our guests.

Data We Collect

  • Personal details provided during reservations
  • Contact information such as email and phone number
  • Technical data required for website functionality
Important GDPR notice: We never sell, misuse, or share your personal information with third parties.

General Policy

Our refund policy is designed to maintain high service standards while ensuring fairness for our customers.

Table Reservations

Reservations made through our website or by phone do not require advance payment unless stated otherwise for special events or private dining.

Prepaid Events & Special Menus

For special events, tasting menus, or prepaid reservations:

  • Cancellations made at least 48 hours before the event may be eligible for a refund.
  • Cancellations within 48 hours of the event are non-refundable.
  • No-shows are not eligible for refunds.

Food & Beverage

  • Prepared food and beverages are non-refundable once served.
  • Please inform our staff immediately if there is any issue with your order.

Gift Cards & Vouchers

Gift cards and vouchers are non-refundable and cannot be exchanged for cash. Expiration dates may apply.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds will be processed using the original payment method. Processing times depend on your bank or provider.
