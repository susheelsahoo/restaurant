General Policy

At Tifliso Georgian Restaurant, customer satisfaction is very important to us. Our refund policy is designed to be fair and transparent while maintaining high service standards.

Table Reservations

Reservations made through our website or by phone do not require advance payment unless otherwise stated for special events or private dining. Therefore, standard table reservations are not subject to refunds.

Prepaid Events & Special Menus

For special events, tasting menus, or prepaid reservations:

  • Cancellations made at least 48 hours before the event may be eligible for a refund.
  • Cancellations within 48 hours of the event are non-refundable.
  • No-shows are not eligible for refunds.

Food & Beverage

Due to the nature of food preparation and hygiene standards:

  • Prepared food and beverages are non-refundable once served.
  • If you experience an issue with your order, please inform our staff immediately so we can resolve it.

Gift Cards & Vouchers

Gift cards and vouchers are non-refundable and cannot be exchanged for cash. They may be subject to expiration dates as specified at the time of purchase.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds (if applicable) will be processed using the original payment method. Processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.
